ii. Question · On the rhythm of remote work

How often do you work remotely, and when do you go into the office?

First attempt · 02 errors

"I work remotely four days in weekend, and I go into the office at Friday."

↦
Polished · iteration 04

"I work remotely four times a week, and I go into the office on Friday."

IV. Pattern library · 文法範式

Three patterns,
committed to muscle memory.

№ i. Prepositions

Time, measured by three words.

時間介系詞 · at · on · in

AT → clock time
ON → day, date
IN → month, year, season
at 7 AM · at noon
on Friday · on April 5th
in 2026 · in summer
№ ii. Frequency

How often, counted aloud.

頻率表達 · N times a 週期

Number + times + a + unit
Use "a" — never "in"!
once a day
twice a week
four times a week
three times a month
№ iii. By + V-ing

Method, expressed in motion.

動名詞表方法 · 用某方式做某事

After "by" → always V-ing
All items in list must match
I relax by reading.
I unwind by watching Netflix,
playing badminton, or
singing.
