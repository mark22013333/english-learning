## 📖 3. Useful Grammar Structures

### Talking about habits — Present Simple

**Example sentences:**
- I usually work from home three days a week.
- I check emails every morning.
- I often take a walk during lunch.

### Frequency Expressions 頻率副詞

| Frequency | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| **usually** | 通常 (high frequency) |
| **often** | 經常 |
| **sometimes** | 有時候 |
| **rarely** | 很少 |

### Daily Routine Template 日常作息模板

> I usually wake up at **7:00 AM** and check my emails. Then, I take my kids to school. I start my work at **9:00 AM**. I often have/eat lunch at **12:00 noon**. I get back to work at **1:00 PM** and finish at **6:00 PM**. Then, I have dinner with my family.

**Afternoon routine:** 1:00 PM – 6:00 PM

**Weekends template:**
> I usually wake up at... I take my kids to the park. Or sometimes, I do some household chores like cleaning and doing laundry.

>
> **Teacher:** Do you prefer working from home or in the office?
> **Mark:** I like both. At home I'm more productive, but the office is better for meetings.
>
> **Teacher:** What do you usually do on weekends?
> **Mark:** I try to disconnect from work. I spend time with family or go hiking.

**💡 Hybrid work set-up:**
- = work from home and go to the office once or twice a week
- = mix of work from home and work in the office

## 📰 5. Reading Article: Hybrid Work — A New Daily Routine

> In recent years, many companies have adopted hybrid working models. Employees divide their time between working from home and going to the office. This change has created new daily routines for workers.
>
> For many people, mornings at home are more relaxed because they do not need to commute. Instead of spending an hour in traffic, they can begin their day earlier and more calmly. Workers often start their day by checking emails, planning tasks, and joining online meetings.
>
> However, hybrid work also requires **strong time management skills**. Without a manager nearby, employees must organize their own schedules and stay focused on their tasks.
>
> Weekends have also changed for many workers. Because remote work can **blur** the line between work and personal life, employees must make a **conscious effort** to disconnect from work and relax.
>
> Overall, hybrid work offers **flexibility**, but it also requires **discipline** and good communication.

**💡 Key Vocabulary from Article:**
- `blur` = not clear (模糊)
- `conscious effort` = mindful / aware / you know what you are doing
- `flexibility` = 彈性
- `discipline` /di-si-plin/ = 紀律

**💡 Pronunciation Tips (字尾發音):**
| Suffix | Sound | Example |
|--------|-------|---------|
| -ty | tee | technology |
| -ly | lee | knowledge (sounds like /ledge/) |
| -gy | gee | technology, biology, psychology |
| -y | ee | technology → tek-no-lo-**gee** |

## ❓ 6. Comprehension Questions 理解問題

| Question | My Answer |
|----------|-----------|
| What is hybrid work? | Hybrid work is a working model by dividing the time between working from home and going to the office. |
| What is your hybrid work schedule? | I work from home from Monday to Thursday and go to the office on Friday. |
| Why are mornings more relaxed for remote workers? | Yes, it's relaxed for me because I don't need to rush to the office. |
| What skills are important for hybrid workers? | It's important to have strong time management skills. |
| Why is it important? | Because you need to be organized and stay focused on the tasks. |
| Why can hybrid work blur work-life boundaries? | Some employees don't follow work schedule because there's no manager and no strong time management. 有些員工不遵守工作時間表，因為沒有經理，也沒有有效的時間管理。 |
| What advantage does hybrid work provide? | It can provide flexible time and save time travelling from home to work. |

**Note:** `advantage` = 優勢
