# Frequency Expressions 頻率表達

> How to talk about how often something happens.

---

## 🎯 Two Main Ways to Express Frequency

### 1. Frequency Adverbs (usually, often, etc.)
### 2. Frequency Numbers (N times a week, etc.)

---

## 📊 1. Frequency Adverbs

| Adverb | Meaning | Approximate % |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| always | 總是 | 100% |
| **usually** | 通常 | ~90% |
| **often** | 經常 | ~70% |
| **sometimes** | 有時候 | ~50% |
| **rarely** | 很少 | ~10% |
| never | 從不 | 0% |

### Where to place them? 放在哪裡？

**Position Rule:** Frequency adverbs go **BEFORE the main verb**, **AFTER "be" verbs**.

#### ✅ Before main verbs (动作动词前)

- I **usually** wake up at 7 AM.
- I **often** take a walk during lunch.
- I **sometimes** work from home.
- I **rarely** go to the gym.

#### ✅ After "be" verbs (be 动词后)

- I am **usually** tired on Mondays.
- She is **often** late.
- It is **sometimes** hot in April.

#### ✅ With auxiliary verbs → between aux and main verb

- I have **usually** finished by 6 PM.
- I will **sometimes** check emails on weekends.

---

## 🔢 2. Frequency Numbers — "N times a Period"

### The Pattern 🎯

> **數字 + times + a + 時間單位**

| Frequency | English |
|-----------|---------|
| 一天一次 | **once** a day |
| 一天兩次 | **twice** a day |
| 一天三次 | **three times** a day |
| 一週一次 | once a week |
| 一週三次 | three times a week |
| **一週四次** | **four times a week** |
| 一個月兩次 | twice a month |
| 一年一次 | once a year |

### ⚠️ Important Rules

1. **Use "a" not "in"**
   - ❌ "four times **in** a week"
   - ❌ "four times **in** week"
   - ❌ "four times **on** week"
   - ✅ "four times **a** week"

2. **Use "once" and "twice" for 1 and 2 times** (not "one time" / "two times")
   - ❌ "I go once one time a day" → stilted
   - ✅ "I go **once** a day"
   - ✅ "I go **twice** a week"
   - ✅ "I go **three times** a month" (從 3 開始用 "N times")

3. **"a" can be replaced with "per" (more formal)**
   - "four times **a** week" (casual)
   - "four times **per** week" (formal/technical)

---

## 💬 Example Answers (from my practice)

### How often do you work remotely?
- ✅ I work remotely **four times a week**.
- ✅ I work remotely **three to four times per week**.

### How often do you go to the gym?
- I go to the gym **twice a week**.
- I rarely go to the gym. I prefer working out at home.

### How often do you see your family?
- I see my family **every day** because I live with them.
- I see my parents **once a month**.

---

## 🔄 Alternative Ways to Express Frequency

| Alternative | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| **every + time unit** | every day, every Monday, every weekend |
| **on + plural day** | on Mondays (= every Monday), on weekends |
| **N day(s) a week** | three days a week |
| **most + time units** | most days, most weekends |

**Examples:**
- I work from home **three days a week**.
- I go to the office **every Friday**.
- I work out **on weekends**.
- I drink coffee **every morning**.

---

## 🎯 Question Patterns

### How often questions

| Question | Example Answer |
|----------|----------------|
| How often do you + verb? | I + (frequency adverb) + verb |
| How often do you work remotely? | I **usually** work remotely. / I work remotely **four times a week**. |
| How often do you eat out? | I eat out **twice a month**. |
| How often do you travel? | I **rarely** travel. |

---

## 📌 Combining Both Types (Advanced)

You can use both in one sentence for natural speech:

> "I **usually** work from home, but **sometimes** I go into the office **twice a week**."

---

## ❌ Common Mistakes for Me

1. ❌ "four times in week" → ✅ "four times **a** week"
2. ❌ "four days in weekend" → ✅ "four days **a** week" (weekend 只有 2 天！)
3. ❌ "I am usually work" → ✅ "I **usually** work" (usually 在動詞前，不是 be 後面)
