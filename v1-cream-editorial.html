II. DISPATCH № 001 — A FIELD REPORT

Three questions. Six iterations.
One perfected sentence.

Session Log
19.04.26
Sunday · Banqiao, Taipei
Unit 2 Speaking Hint-Led Self-Correction

"接受 hints 並自己修正 — 這是最有效的學習方式。"

01

How's your schedule today? Are you busy or relaxing?

First Attempt
"My today schedule is take my kids go to school, and then go to office do work, easy say is busy."
→
Final, Polished
"Today, I take my kids to school, then go to the office to work, so basically I'm busy."
WORD ORDER ARTICLE · the PREPOSITION · to COMMA SPLICE → so "do work" → "work"
02

How often do you work remotely, and when do you go into the office?

First Attempt
"I work remotely four days in weekend, and I go into the office at Friday."
→
Final, Polished
"I work remotely four times a week, and I go into the office on Friday."
N times a week ON Friday AT · ON · IN rules
03

When do you disconnect from work? And how do you unwind?

First Attempt
"I'm after work at 6 PM. I unwind just like watch Netflix and play badminton or singing."
→
Final, Polished
"I disconnect from work at 6 PM. I unwind by watching Netflix, playing badminton, or singing."
by + V-ing PARALLEL STRUCTURE LIST PUNCTUATION OXFORD COMMA

IV. THE PATTERN LIBRARY — 文法範式

Three patterns,
committed to memory.

PATTERN · 01

Prepositions of time.

時間介系詞 · at · on · in
AT → clock time
ON → day / date
IN → month / year / season
at 7 AM · at noon
on Friday · on April 5th
in 2026 · in summer
PATTERN · 02

Frequency, counted.

頻率表達 · N times a 週期
Number + times + a + unit
Use "a" — never "in"!
once a day
twice a week
four times a week
three times a month
PATTERN · 03

By + V-ing.

動名詞表方法 · 用某方式做某事
After "by" → always V-ing
All items in list must match
I relax by reading.
I unwind by watching Netflix,
playing badminton, or
singing.
