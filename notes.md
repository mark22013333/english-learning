# Unit 1: Self-Introduction 自我介紹

**Status:** ✅ Completed
**Level:** A1
**Teacher:** Kiddy

---

## 🎯 Lesson Objectives

- Introduce themselves confidently
- Talk about their job and responsibilities
- Say what technologies they use
- Talk about experience and goals
- Ask simple follow-up questions

## 🗣️ Part 2: Introducing Yourself

### Practice 1 (Basic)

> Hello. My name is Mark.
> I am a software engineer.
> I work for a tech company. *(Software Tech Department)*
> I develop web applications.
> I have three years of experience.
> Nice to meet you.

### Practice 2 (Extended)

> Hello, my name is Mark.
> I'm a software engineer at a tech company.
> I specialize in software development.
> Currently, I am working on a cloud project.
> I have about five years of experience in this field.
> I enjoy solving technical problems and working with my team.
> My goal is to improve my skills and become a senior engineer.
> Nice to meet you.

**💡 Vocabulary Notes:**
- `concentrate` = focus = 集中
- `alone` = by yourself
- `cloud-based` = one drive, google drive, iCloud
- `monolith system` 單體系統 vs. `microservices` (software architecture)

| Question | My Answer |
|----------|-----------|
| What is your name? | My name is Mark / Yu. |
| What do you do? | I am a **Freelance Software Engineer**. |
| Where do you work? | I work from home under a tech company in Taiwan. |
| What do you develop? | *(to be completed)* |
| How many years of experience do you have? | *(to be completed)* |
| What technology do you use? | I program in Java, Python, HTML, C++, SQL. I work with cloud-based and monolith systems. |
| What is your goal? | *(to be completed — e.g., to get a promotion to a higher position to improve my salary, or to become a Staff Engineer)* |

**💡 Useful Definitions:**
- **Freelance** = working independently handling different projects / tasks from different clients or companies (自由職業者 / 自由工作者)
- **SEO** = Search Engine Optimization

## 🎭 Part 4: Role Play 角色扮演

### Situation 1: International Meeting

> **A:** Hi, I don't think we've met before.
> **B:** Hi, I'm Mark. I'm a software engineer.
> **A:** Nice to meet you. What do you work on?
> **B:** I develop software services for cloud applications.
> **A:** How long have you worked here?
> **B:** I have about three years of experience.

### Situation 2: Online Tech Conference

> **A:** Hello! Can you introduce yourself?
> **B:** Sure. My name is Mark. I'm a software engineer from Taiwan.
> **A:** What do you specialize in?
> **B:** I specialize in backend development.
> **A:** What is your goal this year?
> **B:** My goal is to improve my English and technical skills.

## 🚀 Part 5: Upgrade Practice — Transition Phrases

- **Currently…** (目前)
- **In my free time…** (在空閒時間)
- **In the future…** (未來)
- **I'm interested in…** (我對…感興趣)

**Example:**
> "In the future, I hope to lead a development team."

## ⚠️ Grammar Points to Watch (from this unit)

1. **Article "a/an" before job titles:**
   - ❌ "I am software engineer."
   - ✅ "I am **a** software engineer."

2. **"specialize in" not "specialize at":**
   - ✅ I specialize **in** software development.

3. **Parallel structure in lists:**
   - ✅ "I enjoy **solving** technical problems and **working** with my team." (both V-ing)
