## 📝 Question 1: How's your schedule today? Are you busy or relaxing?

### Iteration 1
> My today schedule is take my kids go to school, and then go to office do work, easy say is busy

**Errors identified:**
1. **Word order with "today"** — 應該放句尾或句首
2. **Missing "to"** after "schedule is" (schedule is ___ take...)
3. **Missing preposition** — "take my kids ___ school"
4. **Missing article** — "go to ___ office"
5. **"easy say" is Chinese direct translation** (簡單來說) → should be "basically", "in short", "to put it simply"
6. **Missing subject** — "is busy" → "**I'm** busy"

### Iteration 2
> My schedule is to take my kids the school, and then goto the office do work, basically I'm busy.

**Progress:** ✅ "basically" ✅ "I'm busy" ✅ added "the" for office
**Still needs:**
1. **Preposition after "take":** "take my kids **to** school"
2. **"goto" is not one word:** "go to"
3. **Two verbs can't sit together:** "the office do work" → "the office **and** do work" OR "the office **to** do work" (purpose)

### Iteration 3
> Today, I take my kids to school, then go to the office to do work, basically I'm busy.

**Progress:** ✅ Everything from above corrected! User chose "to" (purpose).
**Still needs:**
1. **"do work" is redundant** — "work" is already a verb → just use "work"
2. **Comma splice** — "work, basically I'm busy" → use **so** / **.** / **—**

### Iteration 4
> Today, I take my kids go to school, then go to the office to work, so basically I'm busy.

**Progress:** ✅ "to work" ✅ "so basically"
**Regressed:** "take my kids **go to** school" — accidentally added extra "go"
**Key lesson:** "take" already means 帶某人去某處 → doesn't need "go"

### ✅ Final (Iteration 5)
> **Today, I take my kids to school, then go to the office to work, so basically I'm busy.**

### 🎯 Patterns Learned
| Error Type | Pattern |
|-----------|---------|
| Preposition | take someone **to** a place |
| Word order | "today" 通常放句尾或句首 |
| Article | go to **the** office |
| Redundant verb | "take" already includes "go" |
| Comma splice | 用 **so** / **.** / **—** 連接兩個完整句子 |
| Redundant verb | "do work" → just "work" |

## 📝 Question 2: How often do you work remotely, and when do you go into the office?

### Iteration 1
> I work remotely four days in weekend, and I go into the office at Friday.

**Errors identified:**
1. **"in weekend" doesn't make sense** — weekend = Saturday + Sunday (只有 2 天)
2. **Missing frequency pattern** — should be "N **times a** week"
3. **Wrong preposition** — "at Friday" → "on Friday"

### Iteration 2
> I work remotely four times on week, and I go into the office on Friday.

**Progress:** ✅ "on Friday" ✅ "four times"
**Still needs:** "on week" → need the pattern `N times **a** week`

### Iteration 3
> I work remotely four times in week, and I go into the office on Friday.

**Progress:** Moving toward article
**Still needs:** 用對 article "a" 但不需要 "in"

### ✅ Final (Iteration 4)
> **I work remotely four times a week, and I go into the office on Friday.**

### 🎯 Patterns Learned

**Preposition of Time:**
| Preposition | Use | Example |
|-------------|-----|---------|
| **at** | 精確時間點 | at 7 AM, at noon, at midnight |
| **on** | 星期、日期 | **on Friday**, on April 5th |
| **in** | 月份、年份、季節、大時段 | in April, in 2026, in the morning |

**Frequency Pattern:**
> 次數 + times + **a** + 時間單位
- twice **a** day
- three times **a** month
- **four times a week**
- once **a** year

## 📝 Question 3: When do you disconnect from work? How do you unwind after work?

### Iteration 1
> I'm after work at 6 PM. I unwind just like watch Netflix and play badminton or singing.

**Errors identified:**
1. **"I'm after work"** — should use verb phrase **"disconnect from work"**
2. **"just like watch"** — wrong construction → use **"by V-ing"**
3. **Parallel structure broken** — watch / play / sing**ing** (三個形式不一致)
4. **List punctuation** — "A and B or C" mixed awkwardly

### Iteration 2
> I disconnect from work at 6 PM. I unwind by watching Netflix and playing badminton or singing.

**Progress:** ✅ "disconnect from work" ✅ "at 6 PM" ✅ "by watching" ✅ parallel V-ing
**Still needs:** Clarify list logic (A, B, and C) vs. (A, B, or C)

### Iteration 3 (after clarifying "輪流或是選其中兩個")
> I disconnect from work 6 PM. I unwind by watch Netflix, play badminton, or singing.

**Regressed:** Lost "at" before 6 PM, lost "-ing" on watch/play
**Key reminder:** Don't lose established patterns when focusing on new change

### Iteration 4
> I disconnect from work at 6 PM. I unwind by watching Netflix, playing badminton, singing

**Progress:** ✅ Recovered "at" and V-ing
**Still needs:** "or" before "singing"

### Iteration 5
> I disconnect from work at 6 PM. I unwind by watching Netflix, play badminton or singing.

**Regressed again:** "play" (missed -ing) + missing comma before "or"

### ✅ Final (Iteration 6)
> **I disconnect from work at 6 PM. I unwind by watching Netflix, playing badminton, or singing.**

### 🎯 Patterns Learned

**By + V-ing Pattern:**
> 用某方式做某事 → **by + V-ing**
- I relax **by reading** books.
- I wake up **by drinking** coffee.
- I unwind **by watching** Netflix.

**Parallel Structure:**
當使用 `by + V-ing` 時，列出的所有動詞都必須是 V-ing：
- ❌ by watch**ing** Netflix, **play** badminton, **sing**
- ✅ by watch**ing** Netflix, play**ing** badminton, sing**ing**

**List Punctuation — 三個項目格式：**
> A **,** B **,** or/and C

**三個項目 → 兩個逗號！**
- `A , B` → 第一個逗號
- `B , or C` → 第二個逗號（在 or/and 之前！）

- 全部都做 → **and**
- 其中選一個/輪流 → **or**
